About K&A Coffee Truck

Our Story

K&A Coffee Truck was created to make it easier for students, workers, and neighbors in Fort Wayne to enjoy quality coffee without having to sit inside a café. We bring the café to you.

We focus on simple, well-made drinks, friendly service, and a welcoming environment—even if it’s just a quick stop at our window.

Our Mission

  • Serve consistent, high-quality coffee and snacks.
  • Make coffee accessible and convenient for busy people.
  • Support local events, schools, and community gatherings.
